360Giving provide support for grantmakers to publish their grants data openly, to understand their data, and to use the data to create online tools that make grantmaking more effective.

Find out more about 360Giving

GrantNav uses open data published to the 360Giving standard.

GrantNav enables people to search, filter and download across these datasets, for free.
